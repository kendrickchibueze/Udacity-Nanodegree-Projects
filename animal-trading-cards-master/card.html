ForestCow

ForestCow tend to be stocky with long, rectangular bodies. Beef cattle are muscular

  • Scientific Name: Bos taurus
  • Average Length: 103 centimeters
  • Average Lifespan: 72.6 years
  • Habitat:Range Land

Forest cows are one of the most common farm animals around the world, and the English language has several words to describe these animals at various ages. A baby cow is called a calf.
